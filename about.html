    • About Christian Public Model School

    Christian Public Model School is committed to providing quality education rooted in strong moral values, discipline, and academic excellence. Our mission is to nurture students into responsible, confident, and capable individuals.

    We believe that education is not only about academic success but also about character building, leadership development, and preparing students for real-life challenges.

    Our History

    Christian Public Model School was established with the vision of providing quality education to students from all backgrounds. Since its founding, the school has grown steadily, maintaining a strong commitment to academic excellence and moral values.

    Over the years, the institution has built a reputation for discipline, dedication, and student success. Today, it continues to serve the community by shaping young minds and preparing them for a bright future.

    Our Achievements
